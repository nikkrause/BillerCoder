Delivery System Reform, Medicare Payment Reform

The Medicare Access & CHIP Reauthorization Act of 2015 (MACRA) makes three important changes to how Medicare pays those who care for Medicare beneficiaries. These changes create a Quality Payment Program (QPP) designed to:

  • End the Sustainable Growth Rate (SGR) formula for determining Medicare payments for health care providers’ services
  • Create a new framework for rewarding health care providers for giving better care, not just more care
  • Combine our existing quality reporting programs into one new system

These proposed changes replace a patchwork system of Medicare reporting programs with a flexible system that allows you to choose from two paths that link quality to payments: the Merit-Based Incentive Payment System (MIPS) and Advanced Alternative Payment Models (APMs). More information is available on the QPP website.
